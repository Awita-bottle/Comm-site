Terms Of Service

Hi! If you've found my services interesting, here's a few important things to keep in mind before commissioning me!
*IMPORTANT: BY COMMISSIONING ME, YOU UNDERSTAND AND AGREE TO MY TERMS OF SERVICE

General Terms

  1. I have the right to decline any order.
  2. The time of completion of a commission may vary from days to weeks. I will do my best to keep you updated. Rest assured I will complete the commission in less than a month.
  3. I retain the rights to all of my work. Commissions are for personal use only. DO NOT use it for AI related purposes or commercial use without my explicit consent.
  4. In case of commissioning a Not Safe For Work piece, you must be at least 18 years old.
  5. I may post any commissions on social media! I will ask you if you'd like to be tagged in the post or if you'd rather stay anonymous.
  6. Please credit me if you wish to post your commission on social media.
  7. A revision to adjust the commission is included at no cost around halfway through the commission. Please take this chance to make whatever small adjustments like color or minor posing. I will not accept changes that involve recreating large portions of the piece. If i forget something that we agreed on, remind me at any time and I will fix it.

Payment

  1. Payment exclusively via Paypal.
  2. Prices are in USD.
  3. I will receive full payment AFTER THE INITIAL SKETCH that you accept and will then begin working on a piece. The sketch serves as the foundation of which i will complete the illustration. (This includes posing, proportions, perspective and background if it applies)
  4. NO REFUNDS. For both of our sakes, be aware of how much you are willing to spend.
  5. For larger commissions ($50+), you can ask for 50/50 payment, of which you'll pay half at the beginning, and half after completion of the piece.
